Moxy Hotel

WEB + MOBILE APP

THE CLIENT:

THE PROBLEM: Marriot International’s new botique brand, Moxy Hotel, caters to millennial business travelers. Their existing site, while aesthetically “cool”, offered no new features to appeal to their target users. Our team of three was tasked with 1) creating a responsive site that would work well on mobile, tablet, and desktop; 2) catering to millennial international business travelers; and 3) generating ideas for hospitality features, as well as social means for guests in communal spaces to easily connect while still respecting guests who prefer quiet and/or work spaces.

ROLE: Product Designer (Research, UX, User Testing, UI)

TIMEFRAME: 2 Weeks
