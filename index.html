Earthlings are interesting creatures. Living on the moon makes it easy to see things at a macro level. Investigating subjects while completely removing myself from all context. I find both Anthropology & Psychology fascinating and endeavor to understand the root reasons/motivations behind a user's actions. It's always better to see than to be told.

While I'm able to zoom in on as something as granular as a button's placement, I can also go way out to establish the system in place. This can take the form of a Strategist or Service Designer.
